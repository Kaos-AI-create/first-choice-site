Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide accurate insurance quotes and filings across the state of Texas, First Choice Insurance Services collects personal information including identity data (Name, Date of Birth), contact data (Phone, Email, Physical Address), and vehicle/property data such as VINs and driving records.

2. How We Use Your Data

We act as an independent intermediary. Your data is used exclusively to secure competitive quotes from our 20+ independent carriers, process SR-22 reinstatements, and communicate policy updates. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketers.

3. SMS and Communication Consent

By providing your phone number via our quote forms, you provide express consent to be contacted by First Choice Insurance Services regarding your inquiry. You may opt-out at any time by replying STOP to any message received.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and secure filing protocols to protect sensitive data regulated by the Texas Department of Insurance (TDI).
